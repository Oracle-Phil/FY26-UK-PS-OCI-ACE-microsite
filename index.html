About this series

This index is designed for internal enablement and should sit beside the thirteen numbered paper pages in the same series structure.

Usage guidance

Use the cards to choose the right source paper for the situation. Use the theme filters when a stakeholder question cuts across the sequence, for example sovereignty, AI risk or economics.

Keep the route pattern stable once deployed. The paper pages are expected to live in adjacent folders using the canonical numbered slugs shown in the source inventory.

Editorial principles

  • Evidence before polish.
  • Internal enablement, not public campaign language.
  • Credible caveats should remain visible.
  • British English and accessible web structure.
  • No external CDNs, analytics or runtime data dependencies.
